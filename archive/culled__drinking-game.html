The X-Files Drinking Game
Compiled by Matt Curr

The Compiled X-Files Drinking Game (Australian Hardcore Edition)

***play at thy own peril***

Rules: Everytime a category occurs on the screen, you take the designated number of healthy sips from your drink. Last person conscious gets to throw up on whoever they want. Pretty simple really.

Note: Stay away from the goon. Playing with cask wine is NOT a good thing.

Warm up
~~~~~~~~~~~~
if it is Wednesday - 1 drink
if it is not Wednesday - 2 drinks

Opening scenes
~~~~~~~~~~~~
Set in U.S. - 1 drink
Not set in U.S. - 2 drinks
Feature Mulder or Scully - 2 drinks
Someone in the pre-title sequence dies - 1 drink.
Someone in the pre-title sequence dies in The Generic Forrest (tm) - 2 drinks.

Opening credits
~~~~~~~~~~~~
if you see an X - 1 drink
if you don't - stop drinking


Mulder
~~~~~~~~~~~~
Mulder smiles - 1 drink
Mulder draws gun - 1 drink
Mulder fires gun - 2 drinks
Mulder blames aliens - 1 drink.
Needs a shave - 1 drink
Is not wearing suit - 1 drink
Mulder gets beat up - 1 drink (& fret endlessly)
Mulder does a voice over - 2 drinks
Mulder with pornography - 1 drink
Mulder's underwear sighted - 2 drinks 8)~~~
Mulder referred to as "Spooky" - 1 drink
Mulder goes into a dark place alone - 2 drinks
Mulder eats sunflower seeds - 2 drinks
Mulder asks Lone Gunmen for help - 1 drink
Everytime Mulder shouts "Scully!" - 1 drink

Mulder gets called to Skinner's office - 1 drink
Mulder lies in near death state - 2 drinks
"We've lost time!" - 2 drinks. (better give the pilot a miss)
Mulder makes a droll comment ("Mulderism") - 1 drink
Mulder is shown the morning after the night before - 1 drink
Mulder argues "It's within the realms of extreme possibility!" - 1 drink
Someone tells Mulder "You're in over your head!" (or words to that effect) - 1 drink.
Trys to give a wild implausible explaination for a "possible" event - 1 drink
- Scully believes it - 3 drinks
Interogates someone - 1 drink
- Gets angry doing so - 2 drinks
- Beats person up - 3 drinks
- Person is Skinner - Finish drink


Scully
~~~~~~~~~~~~
Scully smiles - 2 drinks
Scully gets beat up - 2 drinks
Scully gets abducted - 3 drinks
Scully drinks coffee - 1 drink
Scully's hair is wet - 2 drinks (& a wistful sigh..)
Scully plays doctor - 1 drink
Scully crosses her arms - 1 drink
Scully has a hair out of place - 1 drink
Scully does voice-over/report - 1 drink
Scully exclaims "Oh my God!" - 1 drink
Scully's underwear sighted - 3 drinks 8)~~~
Has a band-aid on her forehead - 1 drink

Scully gives Mulder her "Mulder, you're talking shit again" look - 1 drink.
Everytime Scully does the "Mulder!!" whine (a tad annoying at times) - 1 drink
Everytime you notice that Scully's breasts are noticeably larger than before she was abducted - 2 drinks
Scully performs autopsy - 1 drink
- Body not seen (has it ever happend?) - 2 drinks
Scully comes up with "plausible" explanantion - 2 drinks
- Mulder believes it - 2 drinks
Scully draws her gun - 1 drink
- Aims her gun - 2 drinks
- Pulls hair aside 1st - 1 drink
- Shoots someone - 3 drinks
- Shoots Mulder - Finish drink


Mulder and Scully
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Mulder rescues Scully - 1 drink
Scully rescues Mulder - 2 drinks
Mulder rings Scully - 1 drink
M or S goes into Quarintine - 2 drinks
Mulder/Scully show badges - 1 drink
Mulder rings Scully at 11.21 - 2 drinks
Mulder sends Scully into danger - 1 drink
Mulder or Scully kill someone - 3 drinks
Mulder or Scully is put in jail - 2 drinks
Scully or Mulder infiltrate someplace they shouldn't be - 1 drink
If they convince security guard/worker they should be there - 2 drinks
Everytime Scully or Mulder get really paranoid about the other - 1 drink

Everytime Scully turns up just a bit too late to witness ufo/aliens/paranormal event - 1 drink
- Mulder witnesses ufo/aliens/paranormal event in question - 2 drinks
Everytime Scully and Mulder show signs of intimacy - 1 drink
- In front of others - 2 drinks
Everytime Scully is in Mulder's apartment - 1 drink
- Scully in Mulder's apartment without Mulder - 2 drinks
- Mulder in Scully's apartment - 3 drinks


Mulder's family/Scully's family
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Mention of family member - 1 drink
First appearance by family member in any episode - 2 drinks
Every time family member dies - 2 drinks

Skinner
~~~~~~~~~~~~
Skinner takes glasses off - 1 drink
Skinner loosens tie - 2 drinks
Skinner has fisticuffs - 2 drinks
Skinner supports Mulder - 1 drink
Skinner does not support Mulder - 2 drinks
Skinner says "You got a *problem* with that Agent Mulder?" - 1 drink

Deep Throat/Mr X
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Mulder puts 'X' on window - 1 drink
Deep Throat cooperative - 1 drink
Deep Throat uncooperative - 2 drinks
Mr X cooperative - 2 drinks
Mr X uncooperative - 1 drink
Mr X puts Mulder in his place - 1 drink.
Deep Throat / Mr X deceive Mulder - 2 drinks
Deep Throat / Mr X meet Scully - 3 drinks
Deep Throat / Mr X: "I'm risking my life talking to you..." - 1 drink.

Evidence destroyed - 1 drink
Evidence confiscated - 1 drink

Flashback sequence - 1 drink
Crappy/tacky dream sequence - 2 drinks (or vomit, whichever feels more natural)

Something nasty lurking in forest - 1 drink
A parasite erupts from someone - 1 drink

"It's no use - we're all going to die!" - 1 drink.
"if this gets out, it will kill/infect everyone" - 1 drink

A Dead person appears - 2 drinks
Internet is mentioned - 3 drinks
A Computer does something computers cant do - 2 drinks
1013 or any other sequence adding up to 5 shows up - 2 drinks

Anyone says anything about a conspiracy theory - 2 drinks
The Crew-cut Man kills someone - 1 drink
Frohike remarks on Scully - 2 drinks

Anytime more than one of a character appears (counts if on mobile phone) - 1 drink per additional body
Sherrif Redneck (tm) blows away the suspect before Mulder can interrogate - 2 drinks.
An actor from Twin Peaks appears (other than Mulder) - 4 drinks
You find yourself talking to the character's on screen (a drinking game classic) - 1 drink
You find yourself wildy theorising about aliens/paranormal activities during Ad break - 1 drink
Trying to fill a non-XPhile in on proceedings - 1 drink

Alien abduction - 1 drink

END OF GAME occurs when:

1. Episode is totally closed with no unanswered questions and the
Alien turns out to be a guy in a mask who is haunting the house to lower its
property values. At the end, Shaggy and Scooby Doo help crack the case.

2. Nobody is sober enough to get up and change the video.

3. Aliens start appearing in NYPD Blue. Someone comes up with conspiracy theory involving Jimmy Smits.
