[1] Administration centre.
Also known as "The Faqulty". Steve Leahy, Steve Turtle and Kenneth Alexander Finlayson can oft be found in these offices typing away at their updates to Xreports, FAQs and Websites. Robin Harrison has an office three doors down the hall to the left, where the ATXF Web Site list is maintained. This location also features the ATXF Archives, and the X-Report storeroom.

[2] The Oracle's Trade Store.
Much in the way of Merchandise can be found here, including Rachael Bahl sifting through the magazine racks, and a lost and lonely looking llama.

[3] Quantico House.
A source of much REL. Formerly the residence of Holly "I WANNNA BE SCULLY!!!!" Alexander, currently maintained by Wolfgang "STAY ON TOPIC!! STAY ON TOPIC!!" Schoepf. Most of the walls are covered in X-files posters.

[4] The Weatherhog Institute for Gratuitous Exclamation!!!
[Currently closed] Also contains the headquarters of the now defunct KWAS. Building for lease. Apply to $teve2.01 for details.

[5] ATXF Garrison, Barracks, Blacksmiths and Dining Room.
Home to the various appreciation societies, legions and other organisations that have frequented the ATXF over the years. Currently the location of the Quotewarrior quote vaults. Numerous ATXF regulars can be found lounging about the Garrison.

[6] The Court of the Merchandise Queen.
As featured in several X-reports. Current home of the Merchandise Queen, Annette Fraser, and her court of merchandise fans. $teve2.01 can occasionally be found here, when he's not in the Gutter.

[7] The Battlefield.
The rather charred, crispy and gratified battlezone that hosts the annual FLAMEWAR, QUOTEWAR and currently being repainted for the REL King Championships.

[8] The Gutter Bar.
Notorious public house, famed for the open air atrium which features constant sunlight all year round. The leading cause of TAN and SUNBURN on the newsgroup. Noted local characters include $ciby $teve, and the Robin. Former barman Daniel Frankham was once to have quoted "We are all in the gutter, but some of us are looking at the stars" which $teve took to mean that Daniel was staring at him again.
